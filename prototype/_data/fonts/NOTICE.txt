NanumGothic Font
================

Source: https://github.com/google/fonts/tree/main/ofl/nanumgothic
License: SIL Open Font License, Version 1.1
Copyright: Naver Corporation (https://www.naver.com)

Bundled in this repository to guarantee Korean glyph rendering on
deployment environments (Streamlit Community Cloud) where system
font installation via `packages.txt` (fonts-nanum) may not always
succeed on first build.

Per SIL OFL terms, this font may be freely redistributed with or
without modification, including embedding in a product.
